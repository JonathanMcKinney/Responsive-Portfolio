About Me

I grew up outside of Atlanta in Marietta, GA, until moving to Boone, NC, for college in 2008. I attended Appalachian State University graduating in 2013 with a degree in Environmental Economics and Policy and Sustainable Development.

After graduation I moved to Portland, OR. I was the manager and mechanic for a bikeshop. I spent my free time hiking and exploring the Pacific Northwest. I continue to spend most of my free time riding bikes, rock climbing, hiking, and rambling around outside.

In 2016, I moved to Charlotte to be near friends. I worked for a mortgage company for two and a half years. I had tried to teach myself web development several times, but work always got in the way. I wasn't able to commit the time to reinforce what I was teaching myself, and I decided it was time for a change. In October of 2018 I registered for a coding bootcamp through UNC Charlotte and Trilogy.
